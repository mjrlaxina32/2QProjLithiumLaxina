After Class.

Some students usually hang out in the field after their classes. Some occupy the benches and tables found in the Kalachuchi lane. Frisbees fly around while students walk around the oval. People hang out on the grandstand and the "gazebos." Whether doing reqs, playing sports, or just winding down, you can see PSHS students all over the field. (And of course, externs in the waiting area wanting to get away from Pisay as soon as possible)
